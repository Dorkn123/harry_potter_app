About the Harry Potter Series

The Harry Potter series, authored by J.K. Rowling, tells the story of a young wizard named Harry Potter and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story arc concerns Harry's struggle against the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who aims to become immortal, overthrow the wizarding world, and subjugate all non-magical people.
